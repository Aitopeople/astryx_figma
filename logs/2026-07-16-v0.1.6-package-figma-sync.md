## Intentional Exceptions and Risks

- Inter / Roboto Mono remain the documented Figma substitutions for the official system-ui / monospace stacks.
- Existing official example preview widths are not normalized to 912px when the source layout intentionally uses 640/680/720/1040 widths.
- The user-approved AlertDialog Delete hero promotion and unofficial Card composition section remain explicit exceptions.
- Figma library publishing is manual. Republish is still required for downstream consumers to receive the updated library and to retire stale published entries.
